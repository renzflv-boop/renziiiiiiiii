Humanities and Social Sciences

HUMMS

HUMSS, which stands for Humanities and Social Sciences, is one of LCBA’s Senior High School (SHS) programs offered under the Academic Track. Choosing the HUMSS strand helps students develop strong communication skills, critical and creative thinking, a deeper understanding of society and culture, leadership abilities, and preparation for careers focused on people and communities.

This strand is best for students who are interested in:

-Communication and public speaking
-Society, culture, and human behavior
-Writing, literature, and the arts
-Politics, history, and social issues
-Teaching, law, and journalism

What You Learn in HUMSS Strand

HUMSS focuses on understanding people and communities through subjects like:
-Creative Writing
-Philippine Politics and Governance
-World Religions and Belief Systems
-Sociology and Anthropology
-Communication and Media Studies
-Community Engagement and Social Work

Best College Courses After HUMSS

HUMSS is a great foundation for courses such as:
-Political Science
-Psychology
-Education
-Journalism and Mass Communication
-Law and Legal Studies
-Social Work
-Literature and Philosophy

Future Careers for HUMSS Students

Graduates often become:
-Teachers and professors
-Lawyers and legal assistants
-Journalists and writers
-Psychologists or counselors
-Social workers
-Public relations specialists
-Government workers
